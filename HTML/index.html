About Us

Are you looking for exciting events happening in and around your college campus? Look no further! CollegeCarnival.com is your go-to website for all things related to college events. From sports tournaments to music festivals, cultural celebrations to academic competitions, we have it all covered. Our website features a user-friendly interface that allows you to easily search and browse through a wide range of events happening in colleges across the country. Our website's main feature is students can register in events of other colleges and also can upload posters of events of their college. Motive of the website is to provide a simple registration platform so that students can show their skills in different competitions and can win exciting prizes!
